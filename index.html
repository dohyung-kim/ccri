Interactive Hazard Exposure Map

About This Map

This interactive map displays various climate and environmental hazard exposures affecting children, based on the Children's Climate Risk Index (CCRI) framework. Use the layer control (usually found in the top right of the map) to toggle different hazard layers and their corresponding legends. The map visualizes child population density, the number of overlapping hazards, and population exposure to specific threats.

The data presented is intended to help understand and address the vulnerabilities children face due to climate change and environmental degradation.
